What's Arvopia?

Arvopia is an indie adventure/platformer/rpg game. Arvopia started back in 2017, around November. Arvopia continued on until an idea was grasped. The idea grew and grew, it had gotten to animals, music, and basic platforming by the time it got it's name. Arvopia had reached version 0.4 by that time, and afterwards it grew even more! To the point where we are now, almost a year later. With a fully functional game with platforming, animals, original music, plants, enemies, NPCs, physics, GUI, single layered menu, and all under 20MB!
