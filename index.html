What's Arvopia?

Arvopia is an indie adventure/platformer/rpg game. Arvopia started back in 2017, around November. Development on Arvopia continued from then, and lead to bigger (and better?) updates. Now with creatures that roam the world, villagers that you can speak with, and quests and achievements you can unlock!

Who's Zandgall?

That would be me! Zander Gall, 14 years old and developing video games from the comfort of his 2001 office chair. King of "your mom" jokes in elementary, and proud Minecraft player

Arvopia 0.7

The latest version of Arvopia

Version 0.7 added great capabilities that mostly hide below the surface. Modding support added with infinite capabilities to allow for story lines and completely arbitrary ways to change the game. Many of these things are in the works, such as stories and general modifications.

But this update adds more than just modification support, it also adds player customization, new GUIs, better trading system, fully functional inventory, a full audio system refix ith a new song, as well as many redesigns.

This was an absolutely wonderful update to work on, it doesn't seem to add too much, but it's worth getting.
