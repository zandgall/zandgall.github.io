What is Arvopia?

History

Arvopia started back in 2017, around November. Arvopia continued on until an idea was grasped. The idea grew and grew, it had gotten to animals, music, and basic platforming by the time it got it's name. Arvopia had reached version 0.4 by that time, and afterwards it grew even more! To the point where we are now, almost a year later. With a fully functional game with platforming, animals, original music, plants, enemies, NPCs, physics, GUI, single layered menu, and all under 20MB!

What was the idea?

In the beginning, there was no full idea. Arvopia was just a product of following a small tutorial online. After a while, it then became it's own thing, it was independant.

What is the idea?

Arvopia's future is planned to be full of big updates with at least a few users. Multiplayer and console/mobile support is also planned in the far future. But so far, in the near future, Arvopia is planned to have a 'story mode'. This will keep out the empty feeling of being limitless.

Who made Arvopia?

Alexander Gall, or Zandgall as you should know me. I started Arvopia and still work on it alone today. I independantly run this website and update Arvopia. At the time of me writing this, Arvopia is at version 0.6. It's been a pleasure and a great adventure making (and playing sometimes), this game. Even if there are no players, and even if there will be no players in the next year or two, Arvopia will be a passion project of mine for a long time.
