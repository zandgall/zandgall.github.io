LevelCreator 0.3 - Arvopia v0.5

This version adds a variety of tools. This included the ability to scale worlds much bigger, as well as the inclusion of a +/- control to select tiles. But most importantly this version added a custom entity placement tool, where you could put preset entities wherever you wanted.
