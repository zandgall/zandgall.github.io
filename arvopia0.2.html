Arvopia 0.2!

At least it had foxes, right?

Version 0.2

Released 4 days after the version prior, it's hardly worth a full couple paragraphs written about it. This update felt more like experience gained then features added. As expected for an update in such a short amount of time, there were really only a few additions for this version.

Because this was the first version, it had quite a lot of bugs. One of the main ones, and the most annoying, is a jumping bug which prevented the player from jumping full height 100% of the time. The other main one was a wall clip bug which allowed the player to jump inside of a wall and get launched up pretty high up.

Arvopia 0.1 was just fun enough to continue developing on. After release on March 2nd, pushing it to 0.2 took surprisingly only another 4 days.
