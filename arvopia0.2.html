Version 0.2

Released 4 days after the version prior, it's hardly worth a full couple paragraphs written about it. This update felt more like experience gained then features added. As expected for an update in such a short amount of time, there were really only a few additions for this version.

Like the first version, Arvopia 0.2 was full of bugs, including the jumping bug from before that prevented the player from jumping fully. The introduction of "Lag" came in, the silly little thing that ruins everybody's day, every few seconds a lag spike will usually occur, pausing the game for a short amount of time.

(Broken fox ai)

Other than all the bugs however, the additions added make way for a big section of most updates released afterwards, such as the ability for the player to kill entities. The additions of foxes is also very big from the speed that I realized I would be able to add more entities in the future.
One of the most major additions however is the items and collection which makes up a huge part of the game. Which mainly paved the way for version 0.3.
