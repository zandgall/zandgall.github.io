Version 0.3

10 days after version 0.2, Arvopia 0.3 was certainly a big improvement. Bigger world, better physics, more items, just an overall quality improvement, giving it it's well deserved name.

This update was mostly focused on expanding the ideas set in 0.2. For example, fox fur, honey, and butterfly wings were added as collectable items!

A makeshift 'combat' system was introduced in 0.2, simply click on any entity and it'll die. But in 0.3, health bars were added making the user click multiple times for it's dropped item! Not much of an improvement, but it's something.

Although most of the additions were built off of 0.2, 0.3 started something that, much like the features in 0.2, will inspire a great amount of the future features. GUI's haven't been used in Arvopia until now counting out the simple item counter in 0.2

This version may be near perfect, but it lacks proper jumping causing it to be another annoying version to play around in. However it's definitely worth a shot to play around in.
